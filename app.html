APPLICATIONS4

Healthcare

AI may be applied to: surgery robots, imaging and diagnostic systems, hospice care, biometrics and monitoring, mobile care, prosthetics, and beyond.

Transportation

In the future, self-navigating and self-operating vehicles might be able to communicate with traffic control centers and other vehicles. And safer, more efficient travel may bring about a public transportation boom in North America.

Education

AI is expected to greatly inrease access to education through: remote learning, intelligent tutors, and learning analytics.

Public Safety

Experts predict that most North American cities will become heavily dependent on AI by 2030. Applications include:

  • Surveillance screening for possible crime or drones
  • Predictive policing
  • Detecting white collar crime, such as credit card fraud
  • Predictably, cybersecurity

Workplace

Contrary to popular belief, AI will change the workplace gradually. AI are supposed to replace tasks, not employees--especially since the AI industry is set to generate so many jobs. Ultimately, AI will make conducting business more convenient and virtually barrier-free, encouraging entrepreneurship... or so experts claim.

Military

While we may be dreading the creation of AI soldiers in the future, Big Dog, the headless pack mule that will accompany accompany soldiers through rough terrain, is our only imminent fear.

By the way, self-directing drones, AI snipers, and bomb disposal robots are already in use5.
